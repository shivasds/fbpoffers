Explanation of the offer

Best Price Guarantee

This implies to the lowest negotiated price by the builder during the time of booking (not at the time of visit) Full basket Property will Guarantee the lowest price for the committed project and builder at the time of booking and no caveats are attached to this. Eg - Hypotehtically if a customer visits Project A site in June 2020 and gets a lowest price of Rs.5000 per sft but does not book and comes back in September to book but the lowest price at that time hypothetically is Rs.5300 per Sft then customer will get Rs.5300 because it might be the lowest price at the time of booking. NB - There is difference between quoted price and lowest price. Quoted price is the price which builder gies when a site visit is done but lowest price is the best negotiated price.

Home Loan

0% Processing Charge - Every sanctioned Home Loan attracts a processing fee which has to be paid by the customer before the disbursement. If any customer books home through us in this offer period and avails the home loan through our empaneled banks/agencies then we will waive off the processing fee.

Assured Gifts

On every booking in this offer the customer is assured a gift from Full Basket Property in form a scratch card (online/physical), The Mentioned gift in the scratch card can be availed by the customer after he/she completes the agreement formalities (Registration for Maharashtra region)

Free Interiors Consultation

Full bAsket Property will provide free interior consultation session. This will be done through our empaneled interior consultants. This is a consulting session on approximate costing and design ideas. Full basket Property will not bear any charges of interiors. This offer is only limited to the consultation charges that too from our empanelled interior consultants.

Bumper Draw

Out of all the customers who book during the offer period there will be a Lucky draw among them and one lucky family will be eligible for an international trip. Destination will be declared at a later point of time. Family of 3 (2 adults and one child) would eligible.
